Pejabat Pendidikan Daerah Kluang (PPD Kluang)

The Kluang District Education Office (PPD Kluang) is an institution under the Ministry of Education Malaysia, responsible for managing education-related matters in the Kluang district, Johor. It serves as a link between the Johor State Education Department and schools within the district.

Key Roles of PPD Kluang:

Academic Management:

1. Overseeing and improving academic performance in schools through curriculum and co-curriculum development programs.

Professional Support:

2. Providing guidance and training for teachers and school staff.

Resource Management:

3. Managing workforce needs, infrastructure, and teaching materials for schools.

Community Engagement:

4. Building collaboration between schools, parents, and the local community to enhance educational development.

Kluang district is known for its variety of quality schools, including primary, secondary, and high-performing schools. PPD Kluang aims to ensure that all students have access to inclusive and quality education in line with the nation's educational policies.
